## Research Topics

- **Subjects** (objects, people, environments, places, etc.) and their
- **Affective information** (impressions, preferences, adjectives, onomatopoeia, etc.) ---
- **Measure / Compare / Amplify / Reduce / Adjust** these qualities, and
- **Consider means of reproduction / expression** (media / content)

## Specific Research Keywords

1. **"Multimodal/Crossmodal Affectivity"**
   Extension and transformation of affective experiences through multisensory integration and cross-sensory interaction.
2. **"Enactive Affectivity"**
   Construction and analysis of environments where sensory interest and reflective ("thought-provoking") interest are explored and emerge through the experiencer's actions (interaction with devices).
3. **"Digital Kineticism"**
   Construction of environments where materialized information, informatized matter, and experiencers mutually interact and co-constitute through "movement" as a medium.
4. **"Hapsonic Art"**
   Exploration and practice of the significance of tactile sensation in acoustic expression (a derivative theme of "Crossmodal Affectivity").

::: tip
Research is not limited to the above topics --- we cover a wide range of research contributing to affective informatics, perceptual psychology, media art, and related fields.
:::
